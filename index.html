About Me

My career experience is highlighted by building and scaling impactful HR teams for global companies and providing cost-saving consultation for Fortune 500 organizations based on data driven insights. With a track record for expeditiously developing impactful teams and executing effective program management, throughout my career I have been entrusted with accountability for navigating new environments swiftly and developing programs and processes tailored to the unique organization.

Most recently, career has led me to hold leadership roles in People Analytics, Workforce Planning & Analytics, and HR Tech. I have extensive experience building and scaling impactful HR functions for global organizations and a passion for storytelling with data to help drive informed business decisions. My prior experience as a statistical and economic consultant supporting Fortune 500 companies includes a diverse range of industries (including, but not limited to retail, tech, hospitality, and medical) for employment and labor matters.
