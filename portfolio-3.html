Talent Acquisition

Initiatives and Projects within Talent Acquisition

  • Talent operations metrics development to inform and track operational excellence and efficiency within the talent acquisition function. Design and continuous improvement of Tableau dashboards for TA leadership utilization.

  • Created forecasting models for future hiring demand by quarter and for future years to inform TA staffing planning and improve budget efficiency.

  • Developed labor market reports for all business verticals using a bespoke blend of data from the Bureau of Labor Statistics, LinkedIn, and internal ATS data. Reports were leveraged to inform location strategy and DEI strategies related to talent acquisition.

  • Optimized TA advertising and sourcing budgets by measuring quality of candidates by source to identify best value channels.

  • Built model to identify candidate characteristics that are more likely to indicate higher individual revenue generation.
